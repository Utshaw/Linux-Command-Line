'cat file.txt' prints the file.txt texts in terminal
'cat file1.txt file2.txt'  prints combined texts of two files
'cat -b file.txt' adds linenumber to non-blank lines
'cat -n file.txt' adds linenumber to all lines
'cat -s file.txt' suppress multiple blank line with one 
'cat -E file.txt' replace each blank line with $ symbol
